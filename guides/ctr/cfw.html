SpotPass Archival Project / 3DS Guide (CFW)

Prerequisites:

  • Any modded 2DS/3DS running Luma3DS CFW
    • To check whether or not you have a CFW, while your system is off, hold down SELECT and then press the POWER button. If some text immediately appears on the top screen, then you have a CFW. Otherwise, that means you don't have a CFW and you need to go back and select a non-CFW option.
  • An SD card
  1. Download the latest release of SpotPassDumper11 from the Universal Updater app on your system. Choose the .cia file if you wish to run it from your HOME Menu, or the .3dsx file if you wish to run it from your Homebrew Launcher.
    • You can also download it directly from GitHub if you don't have Universal Updater.
  2. Open SpotPassDumper11 from either your HOME Menu or the Homebrew Launcher.
  3. Wait for the tool to do its thing. Information will be displayed on the bottom screen.
  4. If your system is connected to the internet, SpotPassDumper11 will attempt to upload your SpotPass cache directly to us. If it is unable to upload your cache to the internet, the partitionA.bin file(s) will be located on your SD/microSD card in the /spotpass_cache/ folder.
  5. If your system was unable to upload your dump, you can also submit it using the green link below.
